Our Story

Mirona Industry PLC

Established in 2004 E.C, Mirona Industry PLC is a leading sweet food manufacturer located in the Oromia Region around Sendafa City. Specializing in high-quality GUM and CANDY production, we've been delighting Ethiopian consumers for nearly two decades.

With continuous investment in modern technology and a commitment to excellence, Mirona Industry is expanding its production capacity and market reach while maintaining the highest standards of quality and food safety.

Sweet Food Manufacturing

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's most trusted sweet food manufacturer, recognized for producing safe, affordable, and high-quality GUM and CANDY products that bring joy to customers nationwide.

Our Mission

To consistently deliver exceptional products using modern technology, skilled workforce, and efficient systems while upholding ethical practices, sustainability, and unwavering customer satisfaction.
